Who We Are

We’re “Helping Hand”, and we’ve been “helping people who are fighting with the current situation of Covid-19”. This website is free and helpful for everyone. We are a team of two and recently started this organization to help people all over the state and country.
Our main motive is to reach out to those people who are affected by this Covid disease.
In this pandemic, many people are quarantined at their homes and they can't afford food or daily essentials requirements. So we came with a new idea to make a connection between those who want to help those people and those who need help.

How We Will Help You

Yes, the concept is very simple. Various Food suppliers will enroll themselves in this platform and covid-fighters will also have to register themselves in this platform. Then you can find out the suppliers in your area within a range of 15 to 30 km. So by this, we will know who needs help with food and other essentials.
Don't need to worry our technical team is always available for technical support. If any one having a problem regarding registration or log in or booking services or contacting the supplier we are the middle man to help you out.
There requires no extra charge for registration or membership. Just create your account once and take or give help for a lifetime.
